About

As a mostly self-educated citizen of the world in 2020, confusion and doubt were and are my primary modes of being. This repository was designed as a place for me to make sense of the world. It in no way represents any clear opinions, divisions , or assumptions that I hold on any given topic. My hope is that it will serve as an excellent resource for comparing viewpoints and researching the changing landscape of information delivery. As this was created as mere meditation, and a way for me to learn web design principles, I welcome any and all feedback. If you would like to reach out to me please use the form below.
